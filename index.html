Egor Rakitin, known as Big Baby Tape, was born on January 5, 2000, in Moscow. His musical journey began in early childhood ‚Äì at age 5, he was already showing interest in hip-hop culture.

At 13, he started writing his first lyrics and experimenting with beats. He recorded his first tracks under the alias BJORK, drawing inspiration from the American trap scene and classic hip-hop.

Fun fact: in school, Egor learned English through rap by translating lyrics of his favorite artists. This helped him develop a unique style where Russian and English phrases blend naturally.

By age 15, he had a home studio where he recorded his first serious works. He bought his first microphone with money from selling his Nike Air Max sneakers.
